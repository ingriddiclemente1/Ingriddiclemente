PDA Ginga

The PDA Ginga photographed above

The PDA Ginga was a apart of the Player Development Academy in New Jersey. The club competed in many elite tournaments and every player continued there soccer career to the division I level. The focus of the club was on technical and tactical skills, while preparing players for the collegiate level. At the end of the 2013 season, the PDA Ginga was ranked #1 in the nation among girls youth soccer.
